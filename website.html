          About us

          Bigryo banayo is an online app to share emergency situation and find the perfect service for repairing their automobiles. Each User can share their emergency situation and then the mechanics will be available in their area. The user has to enter his/her location and full details while signing up and also the user has to enter the location while posting their problems or has to send the GPS location of him/her so that it will be easy for us to trace.

          Every user are free sign up in the application. The registered user has his/her own profiles and they can upload the photo and details. The unregistered user cannot access the app. The user can also sign in this app using Google account and Facebook account.

          There are people who need of help for their vehicles to be repaired in any location where it stops working and the mechanics who are looking for problems to solve for their daily living but the problem is that there is gap between them. This app removes a gap and creates a link between them.

          This application is all about mechanical help and getting helped. This application will be a online platform to provide a good facility to people for maintaining problems occurred in their vehicles. The problem of user is send to the mechanics around the location provided by the user.
